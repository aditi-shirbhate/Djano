Flower Waste

Floral waste is one of the major concern. Flowers have applications in many industries viz; perfumes, cosmetics, food, liquor and textile industries. Disposal of flowers in rivers, oceans, etc. leads to water pollution as well as affects the living organisms present in the waters

Innovation

When Agarwal and his partner and childhood friend Karan Rastogi first proposed finding alternative uses for the waste, they met a lot of resistance. The temples thought the young men wouldn’t treat the flowers with the required reverence, or that there couldn’t possibly be a business in flower recycling. Two years on, they’ve proved the naysayers wrong. Agarwal and Rastogi have a thriving company called Helpusgreen
